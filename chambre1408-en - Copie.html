Overview

The Dolphin Hotel has existed for more than one century and welcomes clients from all around the world. When you arrived here, there were no rooms available so the maître d’hôtel decided to give you the room n°1408, the only room available this day. Rumours have been spreading about some strange phenomena happening, and that often, once inside, people never leave the room. “But why should we trust all these rumours?” you thought. Besides, it's not like you have any other option today!

Once you get in, you realise that the rumours were not lies, and that a dark secret lies within this place. You need to escape quickly! But to succeed you must find an exit first, which is not that easy when you completely lose your sense of reality!
